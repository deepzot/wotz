# wotz a kilowatt?

wotz is a prototype web application designed to help "smart meter" electricity customers engage with their energy usage data, using the new GreenButton format. [Try it out](http://wotz.ps.uci.edu/) or watch our [video introduction](http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=0td_CUDtp90).

wotz was developed by researchers in the Physical Sciences and Engineering from the [University of California, Irvine](http://www.uci.edu). The wotz application won the Student Team Grand Prize in the U.S. Department of Energy [Apps for Energy Challenge](http://appsforenergy.challenge.gov) and was showcased at the Sep 2012 Energy Datapalooza in Washington DC.
